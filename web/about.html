ABOUT

Sabor Gandaya (Luis Barron) brings a new approach to the world of rap and hiphop. His lyrics have a deep social message touching on topics of relevance to the current times, more specifically of the Mexico-US border. Rap and HipHop is the avenue to deliver a message that will reach deep into the sole and touch the heart of those who listen. His music will make you dance, sing, feel, cry, and awake to the reality of the world today. His spontaneous demeanor and humble personality will allow you to connect with his music and feelings almost instantly upon listening to his melodies. "This is rap for those who don't listen to rap" as he points out. Rap is where he feels comfortable, but is just the mean to a more important goal: The message of his lyrics. Sabor Gandaya is not just a rapper, he is a social activist in pursue of a more kind and receptive society where equality and understanding prevail.
